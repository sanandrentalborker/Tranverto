Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 8, 2025

At **Tranverto**, your privacy is our top priority. This policy explains how we handle the information you provide while using our services.

1. Data Security and File Handling

  • **No File Storage:** We do not permanently store your uploaded or converted files. Files are processed in temporary memory and are **automatically deleted immediately** after the conversion is complete and you download the file, or if you close the conversion page.
  • **Processing:** File processing happens in a secure, isolated environment.
  • **No Tracking:** We do not track the content of your files.

2. Information We Collect

  • **Usage Data:** We may collect non-personal information such as your browser type, device type, and the pages you visit. This helps us improve our service. This data is collected using standard analytics tools (like Google Analytics, if implemented).
  • **No Personal Identifiable Information (PII):** Since you do not create an account or provide registration details, we do not collect names, addresses, or other personally identifiable information through the conversion process.

3. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to other sites (like social media or external resources). Please be aware that we are not responsible for the privacy practices of these other sites.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: tranverto@gmail.com
